## 📸 1. Why Cartagena should be on your list?
Cartagena is a perfect destination because it offers stunning landscapes and rich culture. If you visit the Walled City, you **will fall in love** with its colonial architecture. This city used to be a fortress, and today it is a vibrant place that you must see!

---

## 📍 2. Where to Go (Places to visit)
You **must explore** these iconic places to enjoy the full experience:

* **Castillo San Felipe:** You **must go up** to the castle to get an amazing view of the sea.
* **Getsemaní Neighborhood:** You should **walk around** the streets to admire the graffiti and meet the locals.
* **The Ancient Walls:** You **can't miss** the chance to walk along these history-filled walls.

## 🥥 3. What to Do (Activities)
There are many activities for every type of traveler:

1.  **Islas del Rosario:** If the weather is sunny, you **can swim** in the crystal-clear waters.
2.  **Local Food:** You **must try** local dishes like arepas and ceviche; they will blow your mind!
3.  **San Agustín Tour:** If you have time, you **should take** a guided tour to learn about the history.
4.  **Salsa Night:** You **could dance** salsa all night in the popular clubs.

![Beach](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1590579491624-f98f36d4c763?auto=format&fit=crop&w=800&q=80)

---

## 💡 4. Travel Tips
Follow these recommendations to have a safe and amazing trip:

* **Protection:** You **have to wear** sunscreen every day to avoid getting sunburned.
* **Safety:** You **must take** taxis instead of public buses at night for better security.
* **Planning:** You **might want to** plan your visits to museums and attractions in advance.
* **Support Locals:** You **should buy** souvenirs in the markets to support local artisans.
